This video presents AUG, a perception-aware UAV planning framework for GNSS-denied, feature-sparse outdoor navigation. It combines topology-guided global routing, robocentric local trajectory optimization, and yaw planning that preserves informative LiDAR measurements for better odometry conditioning. The results show that AUG can avoid perceptually weak regions, reduce drift, and maintain reliable onboard flight over long distances. The video includes simulation and real-world demonstrations that illustrate the method's robustness and practical value.
